# Reviewer Completeness And Risk

Load this skill only when evaluating whether the feature is complete at the full-feature level.

## Required Actions

- Compare delivered work against the original plan.
- Identify missed functionality, incomplete plan coverage, integration gaps, and requirements satisfied only partially.
- Identify edge cases or cross-cutting concerns that were not implemented, tested, documented, or surfaced in verifier findings.
- Classify findings as `BLOCKING`, `WARNING`, or `NOTE`.
